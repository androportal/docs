


sinh
====

hyperbolic sine



Calling Sequence
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~


::

    [t]=sinh(x)




Arguments
~~~~~~~~~

:x,t real or complex vectors/matrices
:



Description
~~~~~~~~~~~

The elements of `t` are the hyperbolic sine of elements of `x`.



Sample
~~~~~~



Examples
~~~~~~~~


::

    `asinh`_(sinh([0,1,%i]))
