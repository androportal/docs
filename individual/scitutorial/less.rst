


less
====

(<) less than comparison



great
=====

(>) greater than comparison



Description
~~~~~~~~~~~

logical comparison symbol

:<> means "different" (same as `~=`)
: :< means "less than"
: :> means "greater than"
: :<= means less than or equal to.
: :>= means greater than or equal to
