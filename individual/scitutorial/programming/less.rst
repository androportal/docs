====
"less"
====

Scilab symbol Last update : 30/05/2006
**less** - (<) lower than comparison

**great** - (<) greater than comparison



Description
~~~~~~~~~~~

logical comparison symbol

**<>**means "different" (same as **~=** )
**<**means "lower than"
**>**means "larger than"
**<=**means lower than or equal to.
**>=**means larger than or equal to
