logical AND operator



Calling Sequence
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~


::

    A&B




Description
~~~~~~~~~~~

`A&B` gives the element-wise logical `and` of the booleans matrices
`A` and `B`. `A` and `B` must be matrices with the same dimensions or
one from them must be a single boolean.
