


tanh
====

hyperbolic tangent



Calling Sequence
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~


::

    t=tanh(x)




Description
~~~~~~~~~~~

the elements of `t` are the hyperbolic tangents of the elements of
`x.`



Sample
~~~~~~



Examples
~~~~~~~~


::

    x=[1,%i,-1,-%i]
    tanh(x)
    `sinh`_(x)./`cosh`_(x)
