


imag
====

imaginary part



Calling Sequence
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~


::

    [y]=imag(x)




Arguments
~~~~~~~~~

:x real or complex vector or matrix.
: :y real vector or matrix.
:



Description
~~~~~~~~~~~

`imag(x)` is the imaginary part of `x`. (See `%i` to enter complex
numbers).
