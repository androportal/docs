====
"imag"
====

Scilab Function Last update : April 1993
**imag** - imaginary part



Calling Sequence
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

[y]=imag(x)




Parameters
~~~~~~~~~~


+ **x** : real or complex vector or matrix.
+ **y** : real vector or matrix.




Description
~~~~~~~~~~~

**imag(x)** is the imaginary part of **x** . (See ** %i** to enter
complex numbers).
