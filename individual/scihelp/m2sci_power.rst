Particular cases
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Note that Matlab seems to have a bug for **X^(Y)** when **X** is a
character and that in Scilab operations such as **X^(Y)** with **X** a
scalar and **Y** a matrix is equivalent to **X.^(Y)** (Will change in
next Scilab versions...).
