


eomday
======

Return last day of month



Calling Sequence
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~


::

    E = eomday(Y, M)




Description
~~~~~~~~~~~

E = eomday(Y, M) returns the last day of the year and month given by
corresponding elements of arrays Y and M.



Examples
~~~~~~~~


::

    eomday(2006,3);
