


.* (Matlab operator)
====================

Elementwise multiplication



Matlab/Scilab equivalent
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
**Matlab** **Scilab**

::

    .*



::

    .*




Particular cases
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

WARNING: Expressions like **X.*.23** are interpreted in Matlab as
**X** elementwisely multiplied by **0.23** while Scilab computes the
Kronecker product of **X** and **23**, to have the same result, insert
a blank between * and **.23**
