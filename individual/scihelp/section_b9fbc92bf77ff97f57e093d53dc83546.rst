


Central Tendency
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~


+ `geomean`_ geometric mean
+ `harmean`_ harmonic mean
+ `mean`_ mean (row mean, column mean) of vector/matrix entries
+ `meanf`_ weighted mean of a vector or a matrix
+ `trimmean`_ trimmed mean of a vector or a matrix
